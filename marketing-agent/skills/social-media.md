# Social Media Skill — Gallery Fotoatelier

## Brand Voice
- Sophisticated but warm — never cold or corporate
- International tone — written in English unless asked otherwise
- Always connect content to the art and the human story behind it
- Tbilisi-proud — reference the city and Georgian culture when relevant
- Premium positioning — never discount language, never desperate selling

## Instagram Post Formula
Every post must have these 4 parts:
1. HOOK — one strong opening line that stops the scroll
2. STORY — 2 to 4 sentences about the work, artist, or idea
3. CALL TO ACTION — one clear next step (visit website, DM us, link in bio)
4. HASHTAGS — Maximum 5 hashtags per post (see HASHTAG RULE below)

## Reel Concept Template
Every reel concept must include:
- Theme and mood
- Shot list numbered 1 to 8
- Text overlays — exact words on screen
- Music mood description
- Duration 15 to 30 seconds
- Hook in first 2 seconds

## Weekly Posting Schedule
- Monday: Behind the scenes or studio process
- Tuesday: Artist or photographer spotlight
- Wednesday: Print or product feature
- Thursday: Exhibition news or upcoming event
- Friday: Photography inspiration
- Saturday: Community — testimonial or collector story
- Sunday: Motivational quote with strong visual

## HASHTAG RULE — NON-NEGOTIABLE
Maximum 5 hashtags per post. Never more.
Always include these 3:
- #galleryfotoatelier
- #photography
- #tbilisi
Then choose maximum 2 more relevant to the specific post only.
Never use long hashtag lists. Never use more than 5 hashtags total.

## What To Never Post
- Blurry or low quality images
- Desperate selling language
- Content not connected to photography or art
- Anything not reflecting premium brand positioning
