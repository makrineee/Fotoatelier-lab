# WhatsApp & Telegram Skill — Gallery Fotoatelier

## Why This Skill Exists
In Georgia WhatsApp and Telegram are the primary
communication tools for business and personal use.
Gallery Fotoatelier must use these platforms to
communicate with collectors, artists, staff, and
partners in the way Georgians actually communicate.
This is not optional — it is essential.

## WhatsApp Strategy

Primary uses:
- Collector personal communication
- Artist and photographer communication
- Staff coordination
- Customer service and inquiries
- Order confirmations and shipping updates
- VIP collector notifications

WhatsApp Business Account rules:
- Use Gallery Fotoatelier branded profile
- Set up automatic away message for after hours
- Create quick reply templates for common questions
- Use labels to organize contacts by type
- Broadcast lists for collector announcements

## WhatsApp Message Templates

Inquiry response:
"Hello [Name], thank you for your interest in
Gallery Fotoatelier. I am Maka, the gallery director.
I would love to tell you more about [work/exhibition].
Are you available for a quick call or would you prefer
I send you more details here?"

Order confirmation:
"Dear [Name], your order has been confirmed.
[Print title] — Edition [X] of [X]
We will prepare your print with care and notify you
when it is ready for collection or shipping.
Thank you for supporting Georgian photography."

Exhibition invitation:
"Dear [Name], I wanted to personally invite you to
our upcoming exhibition [Title] opening on [Date]
at [Time]. This is a very special evening and I would
love to see you there. Please let me know if you
can join us."

Shipping notification:
"Dear [Name], your print is on its way.
Tracking number: [XXX]
Expected delivery: [Date]
Please handle with care — instructions are inside
the package. Enjoy your new artwork."

## Telegram Strategy

Primary uses:
- Gallery Fotoatelier channel — broadcast to followers
- Staff group — internal team communication
- Artist community group — photographers network
- Daily agent notifications to Maka
- International partner communication
- Photography community building

Telegram Channel Setup:
- Channel name: Gallery Fotoatelier
- Description: Fine art photography from Georgia
  to the world. Tbilisi based. galleryfotoatelier.com
- Post frequency: 3 to 5 times per week
- Content: behind the scenes, new works, exhibitions,
  photography inspiration

## Telegram Channel Content Plan

Monday: New work or print announcement with image
Wednesday: Behind the scenes or artist story
Friday: Exhibition news or upcoming event
Weekend: Photography inspiration or Georgian art scene

## WhatsApp Broadcast Lists

Create these broadcast lists:
- VIP Collectors — top 20 buyers
- All Collectors — everyone who has purchased
- Artists — photographers represented
- Press — journalists and bloggers
- Partners — institutions and collaborators
- Staff — team members

Rules for broadcasts:
- Maximum 2 broadcasts per week
- Always personal tone — never corporate
- Always relevant — never send for no reason
- Always give option to unsubscribe politely

## Response Time Rules
- WhatsApp inquiries: respond within 2 hours
- Telegram messages: respond within 4 hours
- After hours: automatic message explains
  response next morning
- Never leave a serious inquiry unanswered
  for more than 24 hours

## Georgian Language Rules For Messaging
- Always offer Georgian language option
- Georgian collectors often prefer Georgian
- Start in English if unsure of preference
- Switch to Georgian immediately if they write
  in Georgian
- Key phrases:
  გამარჯობა — Hello
  გმადლობთ — Thank you
  მოგვიანებით დაგიკავშირდებით — Will contact you later
  გახარებული ვარ — I am happy/delighted

## Privacy And Professional Rules
- Never share collector contact details with anyone
- Never add people to groups without permission
- Always ask before adding to broadcast list
- Keep all financial discussions on official channels
- WhatsApp is personal — keep tone warm but professional
