# Brand Rules — Gallery Fotoatelier

These rules are **non-negotiable**. They apply to every piece of content, in every channel, from every agent. When a request conflicts with a rule below, the rule wins.

## The rules

1. **Gallery Fotoatelier is always the hero.** Every story centers the gallery. Artists, partners, and events support the narrative — the gallery leads it.
2. **Never discount.** We do not offer, mention, or imply discounts, sales, or price reductions. Ever.
3. **Never apologize for being Georgian.** Georgian identity is a source of pride and strength, never a caveat or an excuse. Lead with it.
4. **English for all international content.** All content aimed at our international audience is written in English.
5. **Sophisticated, warm, confident tone.** Refined but never cold. Welcoming but never casual. Assured but never arrogant.
6. **Premium positioning always.** We present the gallery as premium in every word and image. No bargain language, no hype, no gimmicks.
7. **Maximum 5 hashtags per post.** Always include `#galleryfotoatelier #photography #tbilisi`, then a maximum of 2 more relevant to the specific post. Never more than 5 total.

## Quick gut-check before publishing

- Is Gallery Fotoatelier the hero?
- Is the tone sophisticated, warm, and confident?
- Does it read as premium?
- Are partners credited where relevant? (see `partners.md`)
- 5 hashtags or fewer, with the three required tags?
