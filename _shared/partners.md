# Partners — Gallery Fotoatelier

Credit these partners whenever they are relevant to a piece of content. Use the exact names and handles below. When a partner is involved in a project, event, or post, always acknowledge them.

## Partners

- **ReachArt Visual** — Instagram: `@reachart.visual`
- **AFA Art Foundation Anagi**

## How to credit

- On Instagram and other social posts, tag the partner's handle in the caption and/or image where applicable (e.g. `@reachart.visual`).
- In newsletters, press releases, and long-form content, name the partner in full (e.g. "in partnership with ReachArt Visual" / "with the support of AFA Art Foundation Anagi").
- Crediting partners never displaces the rule that **Gallery Fotoatelier is always the hero** — partners are acknowledged within the gallery's story, not above it.
