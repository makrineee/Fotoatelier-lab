# Invoices & Sales Documents Skill — Gallery Fotoatelier

## Philosophy
An invoice from Gallery Fotoatelier is a financial
document **and** a piece of the collector experience. It
must be precise, complete, and tax-correct — while still
feeling as considered as the artwork it accompanies.

## Documents We Create

1. Sales Invoice (final)
2. Proforma Invoice (before payment / for reservations)
3. Sales Receipt (proof of payment)
4. Certificate of Authenticity (accompanies each artwork)
5. Deposit / reservation invoice

## Mandatory Fields — Every Invoice
Every invoice must contain:

1. **Header** — "Gallery Fotoatelier", address (Tbilisi, Georgia), website `galleryfotoatelier.com`, contact email.
2. **Gallery legal/tax details** — legal entity name and **tax/VAT identification number** `[GALLERY TAX ID]` (flag if unknown).
3. **Invoice number** — unique, sequential `[INVOICE NO.]`.
4. **Invoice date** and, where relevant, **due date**.
5. **Bill to** — buyer's full name, address, and (for businesses) tax ID.
6. **Line items** — for each work: artist, title, year, medium, edition number, dimensions, and price.
7. **Currency** — **GEL (₾)** by default; if billing an international collector in another currency, state the currency and the exchange basis.
8. **Subtotal, VAT/tax, and total** — show tax separately. If a margin scheme or exemption applies, state it (flag for attorney/accountant confirmation).
9. **Payment terms** — accepted methods, bank details `[BANK DETAILS]`, deadline.
10. **Notes** — shipping, framing, or reservation terms if applicable.
11. **Thank-you line** — a warm, brief closing consistent with the Gallery's voice.

## Currency & Tax Rules
- Default currency is **Georgian Lari (GEL / ₾)**. Always state the currency next to every figure.
- Always show **tax (VAT) as a separate line** — never bury it in the total.
- Never invent a VAT rate or tax ID. Use `[VAT RATE]` / `[GALLERY TAX ID]` placeholders and flag them for confirmation by the Gallery's accountant.
- For exports to international collectors, flag any zero-rating / export documentation that may be required.

## Certificate of Authenticity
For each original work or edition, produce a certificate stating:
- Artist name and the Gallery as issuer.
- Title, year, medium, dimensions.
- Edition number and edition size (e.g. "3 / 10, plus 2 AP").
- Confirmation of authenticity and that the edition is closed at the stated size.
- Date of issue and signature block for the Artist and/or Maka Buadze, Director.
- A discreet line that the certificate should travel with the work for provenance.

## Proforma & Reservations
- Mark clearly as **"PROFORMA — not a tax invoice"**.
- State reservation period and whether any deposit is refundable.
- State that the final invoice is issued once payment is confirmed.

## Always Include
- Sequential invoice number, dates, full party details.
- Currency on every figure; VAT shown separately.
- Bank/payment details placeholder.
- The standard Gallery jurisdiction disclaimer at the foot of contracts (invoices may instead carry a short "Please confirm tax treatment with your advisor" note where appropriate).
